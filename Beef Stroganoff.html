Beef Stroganoff

Serves 6
Ingredients

Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin steak, 1/2 inch thick
8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced (2 1/2 cups)
2 medium onions, thinly sliced
1 garlic clove, finely chopped
1/4 cup butter
1 1/2 cups beef flavored broth
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
1 cup all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups sour cream
3 cups hot cooked egg noodles
Steps

Steps

1 Cut beef across grain into about 1 1/2 x 1/2 inch strips.
2 Cook mushrooms, onions and garlic in butter in 10-inch skillet over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until onions are tender; remove from skillet.
3 Cook beef in same skillet until brown. Stir in 1 cup of the broth, the salt and Worcestershire sauce. Heat to boiling; reduce heat. Cover and simmer 15 minutes.
4 Stir remaining 1/2 cup broth into 1/4 cup flour; stir into beef mixture. Add onion mixture and remaining 3/4 cup flour; heat to boiling, stirring constantly. Boil and stir 1 minute. Stir in sour cream; heat until hot (do not boil). Serve over noodles.
