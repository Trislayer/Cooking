French Onion Soup

Serves 2-4
Ingredients

Ingredients

1 cup gruyere / gouda
1 cup mozzarella / provolone
3 large red or yellow onions, sliced
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 tablespoon butter
1 tablespoon sugar
Salt
1 teaspoon minced garlic
1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
8 cups beef stock
1 teaspoon thyme
1 French baguette
Steps

Steps

1 Combine oil, 2 tbsp salt, and onions in large pot, cook on medium-high for 7 minutes, stir often.
2 Sprinkle sugar and 1 teaspoon salt. Mix. Add butter. continue 7 more minutes.
3 Add garlic and Worcestershire sauce, continue 1 minute.
4 Deglaze the pot with some beef stock.
5 Add remaining stock and thyme, simmer for 10-20 minutes.
6 While simmering, slice baguette, coat in olive oil, toast in oven at 400Â°F.
7 Top bread slices with cheese, bake until cheese is melted.
8 Top bread with onion soup.
