Le Petit Banana Bread

Ingredients

1 stick butter, melted

1 egg
1 teaspoon water
2 bananas, mashed
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
3/4 cup white flour
3/4 cup wheat flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 or 1 cup chocolate chips
