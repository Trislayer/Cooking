Lentil Soup

Serves 4-6
Ingredients

Ingredients

1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
1 large yellow or vidalia onion, chopped
2 carrots, peeled and chopped
2 tablespoons minced garlic
2 teaspoons ground cumin
1 teaspoon curry powder
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
28 ounce can diced tomatoes, drained
1-2 cup lentils
4 cups vegetable broth
2 cups water
2+ teaspoons salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1-2 cups chopped collards, ribs removed
1 lemon
1 head cilantro
2 limes
1 baguette, cut for dipping
Steps

Steps

1 Warm olive oil in pot over medium heat.
2 Once oil shimmers, cook onion and carrots, stir often, until soft.
3 Add garlic, cumin, curry powder and thyme. Cook 30 seconds.
4 Add tomatoes and cook for a few minutes, stirring often.
5 Add lentils, broth, water, salt and pepper. Raise heat to a boil.
6 Partially cover; reduce heat to a simmer. Cook for 25-30 minutes.
7 Add greens and cook for 3-5 minutes.
8 Remove from heat. Squeze lemon and limes directly into soup. Mix.

10 Top servings with chopped cilantro.
