Meatballs

Serves 4-6
Ingredients

Ingredients

1/2 cup fine, dry breadcrumbs
1/2 cup milk
1 large egg
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1/4 cup finely chopped fresh parsley leaves (optional)
2 teaspoons kosher salt
Freshly ground black pepper
1 pound ground meat, such as beef, pork, turkey; chicken
1/2 cup onion, grated or finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
Steps

Steps

1 Place breadcrumbs in a bowl, pour in milk; stir to combine.
2 Whisk egg in a large bowl until broken up. Add Parmesan, parsley, salt, and generous quantity of black pepper; whisk to combine.
3 Add meat to egg mixture. Use hands to thoroughly mix.
4 Add onions, garlic, and soaked breadcrumbs to meat; mix thoroughly. Do not overwork meat; pinch between fingers rather than kneading.
5 Have a rimmed baking sheet ready. Pinch off a piece of the meat mixture and gently roll between your hands to form 1-inch meatballs.
6 Option 1: Roast at 400°F for 25 to 30 minutes. The meatballs are done when they register 165°F in the middle.
6 Option 2: Bring pasta sauce to a simmer and add as meatballs. Cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes. The meatballs are done when they register 165°F in the middle.
