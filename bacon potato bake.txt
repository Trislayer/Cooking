4 medium potatoes
1 large purple onion
1 tbsp salt
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp garlic powder
2 cups sharp cheddar, shredded
1/2 lb bacon

cook bacon in pan @ 400 until crispy
while bacon cooks
	cut potatoes into cubic inch cuts
	dice onion into 1 inch arcs
remove and pat bacon dry
drain all but 2 tbsp of bacon grease
add potatoe cubes to bacon grease pan, spread grease around
season
cook @400 for 20 minutes
while cooking, cut bacon into pieces
move potatoes around
add onion
cook @400 for 20 minutes
add bacon pieces
add cheese
add green onion if desired
cook 3 minutes
let cool
